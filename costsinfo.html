Costs and Information

TechMUN costs this year will be $20 per delegate and a $20 school fee. This money goes towards conference meals, maintenance, and supplies to ensure the best possible experience for delegates. Payments should be sent in a check mailed to TJHSST made out to TJMUN by March 16th. This is also the last date for any delegate drops with refund.

Rolling Registration will also continue until April 1st. These payments should be sent in as soon as possible after registration, before the conference.

Please feel free to contact us at tjmodelun@gmail.com with any questions you may have. Thank you for your patience and cooperation, and we hope to see you in April!
