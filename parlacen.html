Chair: Ethan Phillips

¡Hola Delegates!
   My name is Ethan Phillips and I’ll be one of your two chairs for the PARLACEN committee at TechMUN 2018. I’m a junior at TJ and have been active in MUN for 5 years now, having had great times at conferences in New York, Philadelphia, and Williamsburg, just to name a few. Outside of MUN, I’m a Varsity Lacrosse midfielder and will play my 7th year on the field this Spring. I am also a very active member of TJ Student Government, lifeguard in the Summer, and spend a lot of time with friends grabbing Starbucks and Taco Bell. I try to catch a few minutes of Caps games too, when I’m not in the gym or studying.
   I am excited to be able to bring this committee to you all and see how you tackle these real world issues that diplomats and politicians struggle with everyday. We want to see good leaders, people who stand out in both diplomacy and authority. If you have any questions or concerns, don’t hesitate to let Otilia and I know at parlacentechmun2018@gmail.com. Can’t wait to meet all of you and start debate in April!

Chair: Otilia Danalache

   Welcome to TechMUN 2018! My name is Otilia Danalache, and I’m so excited to be one of your chairs for the Central American Parliament committee. I am currently a junior at TJ and I’ve been doing MUN for about 5 years. Over these years, I’ve been able to pursue my passions for international affairs and debating while growing both as a delegate and a person. Outside of the club, I’m an officer of 2 TJ service clubs (Keyettes & Send-A-Smile), treasurer for the TJ Speech team, and competing with varsity track and girls soccer. In my rare free time, you’ll likely find me rewatching office episodes, learning ballroom choreography, or petting my friends’ cats.
   I look forward to a weekend filled with innovative ideas and diplomatic discussions as we attempt to resolve these real-world issues. Hope to see you all in April, and again- contact us at parlacentechmun2018@gmail.com if you have any questions!
