Single Delegation


Hint: Down with the Reich!

Director: Kiran Ganeshan

Delegates,
   My name is Kiran Ganeshan. I’m currently a Junior at TJ, and I will be directing Ad hoc at TechMUN 2018. I’ve been doing MUN for four years, but I only started competing in crisis committees last year. Competing in crisis has been a transformative experience for me as a delegate and as a person, as many of you have likely experienced (or will soon experience) yourselves.
   Outside of attending conferences with TJMUN and running crisis trainings as our Crisis Czar, I spend my time rowing with TJ’s Crew team on the Occoquan River and listening to rap music (preferably not Logic).
   You can look forward to an exciting weekend of debate in TechMUN’s most prestigious and difficult committee. Feel free to reach out to me and Will at adhoctechmun2018@gmail.com with any questions regarding committee. Information and clues regarding the committee topic will be sent out over the upcoming weeks so be on the lookout!

Chair: Will Ryu

Hey guys, my name is Will and I'm a sophomore at the College of William and Mary, majoring in International Relations and minoring in Management & Organizational Leadership. I graduated from TJ in 2016 and served as the Senator for TJMUN my senior year. Model UN was one of the highlights of my time in high school, so I’m very excited to return and chair the Ad-Hoc committee at TechMUN 2018! I am now a part of the International Relations Club at W&M and have continued participating in and organizing conferences. I recently served as the Chief of Staff for WMHSMUN, our conference for high school delegates, and previously served as the USG of General Assemblies for WMIDMUN, our conference for middle school delegates. In my free time, I love working on improving my photography, going on road trips, planning travels abroad, and trying new food from all over the world. Kiran and I can't wait to see you all compete in this dynamic and interesting committee this April!
