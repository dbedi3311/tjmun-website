Chair: Pari Parajuli

   Hi everyone! My name is Pari Parajuli, and I’m going to be one of your chairs for Catalan Independence Front. I’m a junior here at TJ, and I've done MUN for about 5 years. Outside of the club, I’m Webmaster for Namaste, Lead Facilitator for KAST, and a Disney enthusiast. I spend most of my free time watching movies with my little sister, painting, dancing, and enjoying good photography.
   The Catalan Independence Movement is a modern day issue that I’m excited to see play out in a joint crisis committee. There are a lot of ways you can take this committee with your thorough (and legible) public directives but I expect political power plays, creative solutions, and Catalan independence by the end of the conference. If you have any questions, feel free to email us at cataloniatechmun2018@gmail.com I’m excited to see what committee will bring in April; see you then!

Director: Niharika Vattikonda

Delegates,
   Welcome to TechMUN 2018! My name is Niharika Vattikonda, and I’m so excited to be serving as your crisis director for JCC: Catalan Secession. I look forward to seeing both committees take decisive action on both of our topics for each side of the JCC. I’m a senior at TJ, and I’ve been part of TJMUN since my freshman year, and this is my 6th year of Model UN. Outside of MUN, I serve as Student Representative to the Fairfax County School Board, as Director of Internal Affairs for Coding Lady Colonials, and an organizer for HackTJ. In my free time, you’ll probably find me rewatching Friends or reading political op-eds.
   For those of you who are new to crisis, unlike your chairs, I will not be serving on the dais, but rather I will be creating crises for committee to solve and responding to your directives behind the scenes. In both public and private directives, I will be looking for creative solutions that approach the issues at hand in unconventional ways and draw from the lessons of previous actions to propose more effective solutions in this committee. For individual delegates’ private directives, I hope to see well thought-out arcs that adapt to the changing crisis environment and ultimately promote your own interests in committee. Don’t hesitate to reach out to us at cataloniatechmun2018@gmail.com. Good luck in your research and I look forward to seeing you in April!

Chair: Shreyas Angara

   Hello delegates! My name is Shreyas Angara and I will be your co-chair for the Catalan Independence Front. I am a senior at TJ and although I joined MUN late in my sophomore year, I’ve loved every conference I’ve been to. Outside of MUN, I don’t really do any clubs due to the early onset senioritis I developed in junior year. However, I do enjoy spending my free time reading books about psychology or economics, rewatching Psych or Friends for the 15th time, or occasionally Python programming for strange tasks. In college next year, I will likely study either computer science or business.
   In terms of committee, I expect delegates to refrain from using cookie-cutter solutions and instead be rather clever in their directives. If you have any questions, please email us at cataloniatechmun2018@gmail.com. I’m looking forward to an exciting weekend, see you then!
