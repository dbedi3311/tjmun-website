Model UN

Model United Nations encompasses a simulation of the UN whereby students engage in political debate to tackle current global issues on the agenda. Based on their committees, students are assigned a delegate and are tasked with preparing draft resolutions, negociating with others, and resolving conflicts while following proper parliamentary procedure.

Leadership

Our delegates possess leadership skills that enable them to command and lead blocs in committee while maintaining successful relations with others.

Public Policy

Delegates inquire about specific public polities and implement various others to directly resolve issues in their committee's agenda.

Diplomacy

Delegates manage international relations through negotiations and handle affairs effectively without arousing hostility.

Research

Students research respective topics for their committee to develop a strong background and foundation leading to success.

TJMUN Goal

To inspire and spread the splendors of Model United Nations to others while enhancing delegate performance..

Our delegates compete at the national level.

Despite tremendous competition and rigor at national conferences, our delegates manage to perform magnificently. Through these conferences, our delegates master effective speaking skills while collaborating with others.

TechMUN is one of the best local conferences.

Our annual conference, TechMUN, is one of the most attended conferences in the area. Months of preparation and planning are devoted towards TechMUN so that it is the best experience for participating delegates.
