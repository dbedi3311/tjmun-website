Chair: Ansh Gandhi

   Delegates, welcome to TechMUN 2018! My name is Ansh Gandhi and I’ll be chairing the Israeli Intelligence Branch, 1960. As a junior at TJHSST, you can find me posted up in my room studying for tests or writing position papers the night before MUN conferences, like many of you will be doing! Outside of school and MUN, I am an active member of Mock Trial and Neuroscience Society, as well as President of HipHopHeads. When I'm not trying to bolster my college applications, I listen to hip-hop, binge on It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia, or read comics.
   You should be well-versed in the powers available to your position and be able to implement them in your directives. Saagar and I will be looking for delegates that are able to work efficiently and diplomatically with others, as well as be able to execute creative solutions in committee. If you have any questions about committee or research, feel free to shoot us an email at israeltechmun2018@gmail.com. I look forward to working with you in April!

Director: Vikrant Magadi

   Hey delegates! My name is Vikrant Magadi, and I’m the crisis director for the Israeli Intelligence Branch, 1960 committee at TechMUN 2018. I'm a junior at TJ, and this is my 3rd year in TJMUN. Between MUN-related activities and stressing about physics, I don’t end up with a lot of free time. The time I do have, however, I spend watching Mad Men, debating Ansh about what Logic’s best album is (Under Pressure), and reading about President Trump’s latest indiscretions.
   I suggested this committee in the hopes that it could provide an interesting twist on the common CIA or FBI crisis. Israel’s turbulent history in the 60’s will definitely inspire some great crisis updates, and I hope it inspires some creative private directives as well. In terms of what I look for, make your directives detailed and in character, and show me something I haven’t seen before. If you have any questions about research or how committee will run, feel free to email us at israeltechmun2018@gmail.com. See you in April!

Chair: Saagar Sheth

   Hey everyone, my name is Saagar Sheth and I will be chairing the Israeli Intelligence Branch, 1960 crisis committee. I’m a junior here at TJ and this is my third year as a part of TJMUN. Outside of school, MUN, other extracurriculars, and volunteer tutoring, I am left with limited free time. During this time, I like to binge-watch shows on Netflix and hang out with friends (without debating Logic’s music).
   This crisis is a historical committee, so you should be well-versed in the events and conflicts of the time as well as your own position. Ansh and I hope that everyone comes to committee well researched and is prepared to rewrite history with creative public and private directives. If you have any questions about committee, feel free to email us at israeltechmun2018@gmail.com. I can’t wait to meet you all and see what committee will bring!
