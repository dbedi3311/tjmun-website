Secretariat and Staff



Secretariat

Alex Lewis

Secretary-General


Hello and welcome to TechMUN 2018! My name is Alex Lewis and I am excited to be serving as the Secretary-General of TechMUN this year. I hope that you are all just as excited as I am to get involved in the group of fabulous committees we have prepared for the conference. Above all, my goal and the goal of the TechMUN Secretariat is to make sure that not only do you learn some valuable lessons over the course of the weekend, but also have a lot of fun. Outside of Model UN, I love to play piano, do community service through the Boy Scouts, and listen to whatever new music seems interesting. We wish you happy preparing, and cannot wait to see you all in April!

Rounak Das

Undersecretary-General


Hope you’re ready for TechMUN 2018! I’m Rounak Das, serving as the Under Secretary-General of TechMUN this year. It’s been a great time working to set this conference up, and I’m excited to see the discussion and cooperation that’ll take place in the variety of committees we’ve prepared for you. Delegates will have all sorts of opportunities to interact with topics they’re interested in, especially through our Crisis organ, which is bound to offer fast-paced challenges. In addition to MUN, my interests include playing the guitar, crossing names off my eatery bucket list, and only listening to musical artists with “Lil” in front of their names. Looking forward to seeing you all in April!

Sachin Jain

Senator


Welcome everybody and get ready for TechMUN 2018! My name is Sachin Jain and this year I have the honor of being part of the TechMUN 2018 Secretariat as Senator. From the UN Children’s Fund to Silicon Valley, 2018, this year’s iteration of TechMUN is sure to be filled with heated debate, effectively diplomacy, and countless opportunities to learn from your fellow delegates. In addition to closing my fourth year of MUN, I compete in Forensic Speech and hackathons. As a huge tech nerd, I’m excited to see what direction you drive your committees and how you take advantage of recent advancements in technology. April 20th is closer than it seems, so while we’re stuffing folders, y’all should get preparing! If you have any conference questions, please email your committees on their specified committee email or us at tjmodelun@gmail.com.

Atharv Gupta

Arbitrator


Delegates, welcome to TechMUN 2018! My name is Atharv Gupta and I’m proud to be serving as your Chief of Staff for this year’s conference. I’m currently a junior and this is my 5th year in Model UN. I also serve on the club secretariat as the Arbitrator (training coordinator) and regularly travel to compete. In my scarce time outside of MUN, I play the trumpet for the our school’s jazz band, listen to music religiously (Logic), and run my own YouTube channel so peep that here: Youtube Chanel
     The few months leading up to TechMUN have been a blur of logistical details, spreadsheets, stuffing folders, and a whole host of other jobs to run this conference. I’m excited to see how our team’s hard work pays off to give you all the best TechMUN experience possible! If you have any conference questions, please email your committees on their specified committee email or us at tjmodelun@gmail.com. See you soon!
